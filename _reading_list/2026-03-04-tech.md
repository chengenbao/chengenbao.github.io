### 1. Teaching AI to Read a Map — Google Research

**链接：** [https://research.google/blog/teaching-ai-to-read-a-map/](https://research.google/blog/teaching-ai-to-read-a-map/)  
**来源：** Google Research · 2026-02-17

Google Research 构建专项基准与 VLM 微调流程，使 AI 真正理解地图符号体系与空间语义，不只识别道路拓扑。在地图阅读任务上大幅超越通用 VLM 基线，对 GIS、户外导航与城市规划具有直接价值。

### 2. Nationwide Randomized Study of AI in Virtual Care — Google Research

**链接：** [https://research.google/blog/collaborating-on-a-nationwide-randomized-study-of-ai-in-real-world-virtual-care/](https://research.google/blog/collaborating-on-a-nationwide-randomized-study-of-ai-in-real-world-virtual-care/)

Google 与 Included Health 宣布全美首个大规模 RCT，评估对话式 AI（AMIE）在真实虚拟诊疗中的效果。超越模拟环境，招募全国患者对比 AI 辅助问诊与标准临床流程，是负责任医疗 AI 落地的里程碑举措。

### 3. CORPGEN: AI Agents for Real Work — Microsoft Research

**链接：** [https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/blog/corpgen-advances-ai-agents-for-real-work/](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/blog/corpgen-advances-ai-agents-for-real-work/)

### 4. Paza: ASR for Low-Resource Languages — Microsoft Research

**链接：** [https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/blog/paza-introducing-automatic-speech-recognition-benchmarks-and-models-for-low-resource-languages/](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/blog/paza-introducing-automatic-speech-recognition-benchmarks-and-models-for-low-resource-languages/)  
**来源：** Microsoft Research · 2026-01

PazaBench：首个低资源语言 ASR 排行榜，39 种非洲语言，51 个 SOTA 模型。Paza 模型在肯尼亚 6 种语言真实场景测试中，以少量数据微调大幅超越通用基线，致力于弥合全球语音 AI 鸿沟。
